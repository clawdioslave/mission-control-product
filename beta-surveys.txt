## 📊 SURVEY SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

### Google Forms Setup:

1. Go to forms.google.com
2. Click "+ Blank" to create new form
3. Copy questions from above
4. Settings:
   - Collect email addresses: ON
   - Limit to 1 response: OFF (they need to take it twice)
   - Show progress bar: ON
5. Theme:
   - Header color: Match your brand (blue/purple)
   - Background: Light
6. Share:
   - Get link (short URL)
   - Set to "Anyone with link can respond"

### Survey Links to Save:
- Day 2 Survey: [YOUR_LINK]
- Day 5 Survey: [YOUR_LINK]

### Response Tracking:
- Set up Google Sheets auto-sync
- Check responses daily
- Reply to each person within 24 hours
