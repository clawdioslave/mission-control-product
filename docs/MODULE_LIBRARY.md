# Mission Control Module Library
## Available Modules for Customization

### 🎯 CORE MODULES (Always Available)

#### 1. Daily Command Center
- **Purpose:** Morning focus and priority setting
- **Features:**
  - 3 MITs (Most Important Tasks)
  - Daily intention/focus statement
  - Quick win tracker
  - Energy level indicator
  - Distraction blocker list
- **Best for:** Everyone

#### 2. Weekly Review Dashboard
- **Purpose:** End-of-week reflection and planning
- **Features:**
  - Wins from this week
  - Challenges faced
  - Lessons learned
  - Next week preview
  - Goal progress check
- **Best for:** Everyone

---

### 💼 BUSINESS MODULES

#### 3. Client Project Tracker
- **Purpose:** Manage client work and deliverables
- **Features:**
  - Client list with status
  - Project timelines
  - Deliverable checklist
  - Communication log
  - Payment status
- **Best for:** Freelancers, Agencies, Consultants
- **Customizable fields:**
  - Hourly vs project rates
  - Retainer tracking
  - Contract dates
  - Client priority tiers

#### 4. Revenue & Finances
- **Purpose:** Track income, expenses, and financial goals
- **Features:**
  - Monthly revenue tracker
  - Expense categories
  - Profit margin calculator
  - Tax estimation
  - Invoice status
- **Best for:** Everyone
- **Customizable fields:**
  - Currency (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.)
  - Multiple income streams
  - Recurring vs one-time revenue
  - Savings goals

#### 5. Pipeline & Leads
- **Purpose:** Track potential clients and deals
- **Features:**
  - Lead source tracking
  - Deal stages (inquiry → proposal → closed)
  - Follow-up reminders
  - Conversion rate calculator
  - Lost deal analysis
- **Best for:** Agencies, Consultants, B2B services

#### 6. Team & Delegation
- **Purpose:** Manage team members and tasks
- **Features:**
  - Team member directory
  - Task assignments
  - Workload distribution
  - Time-off tracking
  - Performance notes
- **Best for:** Agencies, Startups with team

### 🎯 PRODUCTIVITY MODULES

- **Features:**
  - Quarterly objectives

  - Weekly progress bars

  - Celebration tracker
- **Best for:** Everyone
- **Customization:**

  - Personal vs business goals
  - Visibility (public vs private)

#### 8. Time Tracking & Insights
- **Purpose:** Understand where time goes
- **Features:**
  - Daily time blocks
  - Category breakdown (deep work, admin, meetings)
  - Time sink identification
  - Productivity score

- **Best for:** Freelancers, Consultants
- **Integrations:** Manual entry or timer

#### 9. Habit & Routine Builder
- **Purpose:** Build and track key habits
- **Features:**
  - Daily habit checklist
  - Streak counter
  - Habit stacking planner
  - Routine templates
  - Habit scorecard
- **Best for:** Everyone
- **Templates:**
  - Morning routine
  - Evening shutdown
  - Deep work ritual
  - Exercise tracker

#### 10. Energy & Wellness
- **Purpose:** Monitor energy and prevent burnout
- **Features:**
  - Energy level tracker (1-10)
  - Sleep quality log
  - Mood tracking
  - Burnout warning signals
  - Recovery activity log

## 🎨 CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS

### Visual Themes
- **Dark Mode** (default) — Professional, easy on eyes
- **Light Mode** — Clean, bright
- **Color Accents:** Blue, Purple, Green, Orange, Pink

### Layout Options
- **Grid Layout** — All modules visible
- **Focus Layout** — One module at a time
- **Sidebar Layout** — Navigation sidebar

### Data Input Methods
- **Manual Entry** — Type everything
- **Quick Add Buttons** — One-click common entries
- **Voice Notes** — Record thoughts (Pro tier)
- **File Upload** — Attach receipts, contracts (Pro tier)

### Notification Preferences
- **Daily Email Digest** — Morning summary
- **Weekly Report** — Sunday night review
- **Slack Integration** — Team updates (Agency tier)
