# Mission Control Pricing Strategy

## 🎯 Philosophy

**Old Thinking:** Maximize revenue per user

## 🎁 Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Free Launch (Week 1-2)
**Goal:** 500 free users, collect feedback

**Tactics:**
- Launch free tier only
- "Be a founding user" campaign
- Collect testimonials in exchange for free Pro upgrade
- Daily feedback surveys

### Phase 2: Pro Introductory (Week 3-4)
**Goal:** First 100 Pro users at $19

**Tactics:**
- Email free users: "Founding member pricing"
- Limited time: $19 (will be $49)
- Scarcity: First 100 only
- Social proof: "Join 500+ users already organized"

### Phase 3: Full Launch (Month 2)
**Goal:** 1000+ users, 150+ Pro

**Tactics:**
- Public launch on Product Hunt
- Affiliate program (30% commission)
- Team tier introduction
- Case studies from successful users

## 🔄 Feedback Loop System

### Week 1 Survey (Free Users)
**Questions:**
1. What module do you use most?
2. What's missing that you need?
3. What's confusing?
4. Would you pay $29 for automation scripts? (Y/N)
5. Can we feature your story? (Y/N)

**Reward:** Pro upgrade for detailed responses

### Week 2 Survey (Free Users)
**Questions:**
1. How many days did you use Mission Control?
2. What feature would make you upgrade?
3. What other tools do you use?
4. Would you recommend to a friend? (NPS)

**Reward:** Entry to win 1-year Pro subscription

### Monthly (Pro Users)
**Questions:**
1. ROI: Time saved per week?
2. Revenue impact?
3. Feature requests
4. Testimonial opportunity

**Reward:** Team tier discount

## 🎨 Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring
- Show $99 crossed out, $29 current
- Team tier at $79 makes Pro look cheap

### Decoy Effect
- Pro: $29 one-time
- Team: $79/month
- Makes Pro look like amazing value

### Loss Aversion
- "Your free trial ends in 3 days"
- "Founding member pricing expires Sunday"

### Social Proof
- "Join 1,247 freelancers getting organized"
- "Used by teams at [logos]"
