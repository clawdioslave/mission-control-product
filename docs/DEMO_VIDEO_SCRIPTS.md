## 🎬 PRODUCTION NOTES

### Recording Setup:
- **Software:** OBS (free) or Loom (easier)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 minimum
- **Audio:** Clear voiceover, no background music during speech
- **Captions:** Add for accessibility (YouTube auto-captions work)

### Visual Style:
- **Background:** Clean desk or blurred office
- **Lighting:** Natural light or ring light
- **Attire:** Professional casual (what your audience wears)

### Screen Recording Tips:
- Zoom in on important details (CMD + +)
- Use cursor highlight (Loom has this built-in)
- Pause after key actions
- Show real data, not lorem ipsum

### Editing:
- **Software:** iMovie (free), DaVinci Resolve (free), or Premiere
- **Pacing:** Cut dead air, keep energy high
- **B-Roll:** Add shots of dashboard in use
- **Text Overlays:** Key features, pricing

### Thumbnail Design:
- **Style:** Before/After split screen
- **Text:** "Free Dashboard" or "$29 → Organized"
- **Colors:** High contrast, brand colors
- **Face:** Include your face (increases CTR)
