## 🎛️ The Customization Process

### Step 1: Answer 6 Questions (2 minutes)

1. **Who are you?** (Freelancer, Agency, Startup, Creator, Consultant)
2. **What do you track?** (Projects, Finances, Goals, Content, Team, etc.)
3. **What's your biggest challenge?** (Tool overload, deadlines, time tracking, etc.)
4. **Tech comfort level?** (Basic to Advanced)
5. **Business name & your name** (For personalization)
6. **Primary goal?** (Get organized, scale, save time, reduce stress)

### Step 2: Get Your Custom Dashboard (Instant)

The customizer generates:
- ✅ Personalized HTML dashboard
- ✅ Pre-configured JavaScript
- ✅ Setup guide for YOUR situation
- ✅ Custom CSS styling
- ✅ README with tips for your business type

### Step 3: Add Your Data (5 minutes)

Edit one file (`config.js`) with:
- Your daily priorities
- Your active projects
- Your revenue goals
- Your 90-day objectives

### Step 4: Use Daily

Open `dashboard.html` each morning. See everything at a glance. Work from priorities.
