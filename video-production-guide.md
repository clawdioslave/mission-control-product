## VIDEO 1: 60-Second Elevator Pitch
**Purpose:** Hook viewers, show transformation, drive to landing page
**Platform:** Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram Reels

### Shot List & Script:

#### Shot 1: The Hook (0:00-0:05)
**Visual:** Screen recording of messy desktop
- 20+ browser tabs open
- Multiple apps running
- Notification badges everywhere
- Text overlay: "This was my life..."

**Script:**
"I used to run my business from 17 browser tabs, 4 apps, and my head—which was failing."

#### Shot 2: The Problem (0:05-0:15)
**Visual:** Rapid cuts showing different tools
- Notion (messy workspace)
- Trello (too many cards)
- Spreadsheets (overwhelming data)
- Calendar (packed schedule)
- Text overlay: "Too many tools..."

**Script:**
"Notion was too flexible. Trello was too simple. Asana was too complex. Nothing fit MY workflow."

#### Shot 3: The Solution (0:15-0:25)
**Visual:** Clean Mission Control dashboard
- Dark mode, organized layout
- Daily Focus module prominent
- Smooth animation/scroll through modules
- Text overlay: "So I built this..."

**Script:**
"So I built Mission Control. Answer 6 questions, get a personalized business dashboard in 2 minutes."

#### Shot 4: Features (0:25-0:40)
**Visual:** Screen recording showing customization
- Customizer wizard (6 questions)
- Generated dashboard
- Different modules (Daily Focus, Projects, Revenue)
- Mobile view (responsive)
- Text overlay: "Daily focus. Projects. Revenue. All in one view."

**Script:**
"Choose your business type, select what you track—clients, revenue, goals—and Mission Control builds your command center."

#### Shot 5: CTA (0:40-0:60)
**Visual:** Split screen
- Left: Pricing graphic (Free $0, Pro $29)
- Right: Dashboard screenshot
- Text overlay: "Free forever. Upgrade when ready."
- Final frame: QR code or URL

**Script:**
"Start free forever, upgrade to Pro when you're ready. Join 1,000+ founders getting organized. Link in bio."

### Technical Specs:
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 (vertical) OR 1920x1080 (horizontal)
- **Frame rate:** 30fps
- **Background music:** Upbeat, instrumental (Epidemic Sound or Artlist)
- **Captions:** Required (most watch muted)

## VIDEO 2: 3-Minute Product Demo
**Purpose:** Full walkthrough for interested prospects
**Platform:** YouTube, landing page, Gumroad

### Shot List & Script:

#### Intro (0:00-0:30)
**Visual:** Face to camera (confessional style)
**Setting:** Clean desk, natural lighting

**Script:**
"Hey, I'm Chris. I've spent the last 3 months building Mission Control because I couldn't find a dashboard system that actually fit how I work.

Today I'm going to show you how it works, how to customize it, and why it's different from every other productivity tool out there.

Let's dive in."

#### The Problem (0:30-1:00)
**Visual:** Screen share - messy workflow demonstration
- Show 5+ apps open
- Switch between them rapidly
- Show frustration

**Script:**
"Here's my old workflow: Notion for notes, Trello for tasks, Sheets for finances, Calendar for deadlines, and a notebook for ideas.

I'd spend 20 minutes every morning just figuring out what to work on. Sound familiar?"

**Visual:** Screen share - open Mission Control customizer
- Show clean interface
- Point to browser address bar (local file)

**Script:**
"Mission Control fixes this by giving you ONE dashboard that's customized for YOUR business.

Let me show you how the customizer works."

**Q3: What do you track?**

**Q6: Visual style**

**Result screen:**
- Show generated dashboard

**Script:**
"Here's my Daily Focus—my 3 priorities for today. Below that, active client projects with deadlines. Revenue tracker shows this month vs. goal. And my 90-day goals with weekly milestones.

Everything I need, one screen, zero clutter."

$29 one-time, not per month."

**Visual:** Back to face + screen split

**Script:**
"Try the free version—link below. Use it for a week. If it clicks, upgrade to Pro.

Questions? Comment below or DM me. Thanks for watching!"

## VIDEO 3: 5-Minute Setup Tutorial
**Purpose:** Help new users get started quickly
**Platform:** YouTube, help center, onboarding emails

### Shot List & Script:

#### Intro (0:00-0:30)
**Visual:** Face to camera

**Script:**
"In this video, I'll walk you through setting up Mission Control from download to daily use. Total time: 5 minutes. Let's go."

#### Step 1: Download & Extract (0:30-1:00)
**Visual:** Screen recording
- Show Gumroad download
- Extract ZIP file
- Show folder contents

**Script:**
"First, download your Mission Control ZIP from Gumroad. Extract it anywhere—Desktop, Documents, wherever. You'll see dashboard.html, config.js, and other files."

#### Step 2: Open Dashboard (1:00-1:30)
**Visual:** Double-click dashboard.html
- Opens in browser
- Show default view with placeholder data

**Script:**
"Double-click dashboard.html. It opens in your default browser. Right now you're seeing placeholder data—let's customize it."

#### Step 3: Edit Config.js (1:30-3:00)
**Visual:** Open config.js in text editor

**Script:**
"Open config.js in any text editor—TextEdit on Mac, Notepad on Windows, or VS Code if you're technical.

First, daily focus. Replace these empty strings with your actual 3 priorities for today.

Next, projects. Add your active client work with real deadlines and status.

Revenue section—enter your monthly goal and what you've actually earned so far.

Goals—your real 90-day objectives with target dates.

Save the file."

#### Step 4: Refresh & Verify (3:00-3:30)
**Visual:** Refresh browser
- Show updated data appearing
- Scroll through modules

**Script:**
"Refresh your browser. Boom—your dashboard now shows YOUR data. Daily priorities, real projects, actual revenue, your goals."

#### Step 5: Daily Workflow (3:30-4:30)
**Visual:** Demonstrate daily use

**Script:**
"Here's my daily workflow:

Morning: Open Mission Control, check my 3 priorities, scan upcoming deadlines, then close it and do the work.

Throughout the day: Log time as I work, check off completed tasks, capture ideas in the Ideas module.

Evening: Update what's done, note anything for tomorrow, close with peace of mind.

Takes 5 minutes total, saves me an hour of decision fatigue."

#### Outro (4:30-5:00)
**Visual:** Face to camera

**Script:**
"That's it—you're set up! Use it for a few days, get comfortable, then explore the other modules.

Questions? Comment below or email hello@missioncontrol.io.

Thanks for watching!"

## 🎥 SCREEN RECORDING SOFTWARE

### Free Options:
- **OBS Studio** (Windows/Mac/Linux) — Most powerful
- **QuickTime** (Mac) — Built-in, simple
- **Xbox Game Bar** (Windows) — Built-in, Win+G

### Paid Options:
- **Loom** — Easiest, instant sharing
- **ScreenFlow** (Mac) — Professional editing
- **Camtasia** — Advanced features

### Recommended:
- **Loom for quick demos** (easiest setup)
- **OBS for polished videos** (most control)

## 🎯 RECORDING PRIORITY

**Record in this order:**

1. **60-Second Elevator Pitch** (Highest impact, do first)
2. **3-Minute Product Demo** (For landing page)
3. **5-Minute Setup Tutorial** (For support)
4. **Social Clips** (Cut from longer videos)

**Start with the 60-second pitch** — it's your most important marketing asset.
